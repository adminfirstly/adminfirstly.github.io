Adminfirstly Refund Policy

Effective Date: April 09, 2025

1. Refund Application Deadline

Users must submit a refund application within 7 calendar days after purchasing the service/product; applications submitted after this period will not be accepted.

2. Conditions Applicable for Refunds

  • Unused Principle: Users must prove that they have not logged into their account, activated the service, or downloaded/used any features (as verified by system logs).
  • Functionality Integrity: If a technical fault renders the core functionality unusable, users may apply for a refund after verification by the technical team.

3. Situations Not Eligible for Refunds

  • Services purchased at a discounted price or during promotional events;
  • Users who have partially used the service or violated the "User Agreement" (such as account sharing or malicious operations);
  • Abnormal service experiences caused by issues with the user's device or network.

4. Refund Process

Application Method:

Send an email to hello@adminfirstly.com with the subject "Refund Application." The email body must include:

  • Order number
  • Purchase date
  • Refund reason and relevant proof (e.g., screenshots, log files)

Processing Time:

Upon receiving the application, we will review it and respond by email within 3-5 business days.

Refund Receipt:

Once approved, the amount will be returned to the original payment account. The actual refund time depends on the payment platform.

5. Special Instructions

  • This policy applies only to users in mainland China; cross-border transactions require separate negotiation;
  • For service interruptions caused by force majeure (such as server maintenance or policy adjustments), an extension of service may be negotiated instead of a refund;
  • Adminfirstly reserves the final interpretation rights.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please email: hello@adminfirstly.com
(Suggested inquiry hours: 9:00–18:00 on business days; responses within 24 hours)
